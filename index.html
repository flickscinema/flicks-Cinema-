Avatar (2009)

Avatar

2009Sci-fi/Action2h 42m

In 2154, humans have depleted Earth's natural resources. The Resources Development Administration (RDA) mines a valuable mineral called unobtanium on Pandora, a moon in the Alpha Centauri star system. Pandora's atmosphere is toxic to humans, so scientists create genetically engineered human-Na'vi hybrids called avatars, operated by genetically matched humans. Jake Sully, a former Marine paralyzed from the waist down, replaces his deceased twin brother as an avatar operator. Initially sent to gather intelligence that will help the military remove the Na'vi, Jake becomes immersed in Na'vi culture through his relationship with Neytiri. As he learns their ways and falls in love with Neytiri, he must choose between his mission and protecting the Na'vi and their home.

The Odyssey

2026Epic/Drama3h 05m

Directed by Christopher Nolan, this film is a realistic adaptation of Homer's epic. It follows Odysseus (Matt Damon), the Greek king of Ithaca, on his ten-year journey home after the Trojan War. The story explores his cleverness as a strategist while facing the Cyclops Polyphemus, the Sirens, and the goddess Circe. In Ithaca, his wife Penelope (Anne Hathaway) must fends off power-hungry suitors while their son Telemachus (Tom Holland) sets out to find his father.

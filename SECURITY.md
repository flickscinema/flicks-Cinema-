## Reporting a Vulnerability

Use this section to tell people how to report a vulnerability.

Where to Report
Please send all security-related reports to our team via:

Email: cinemaworldofficial123@gmail.com

WhatsApp: +94 75 727 8880

Communication Frequency
We value your help and will keep you informed throughout the process:

Initial Response: You will receive an acknowledgment within 48 hours.

Progress Updates: You can expect a status update every 3–5 days until the issue is resolved.

What to Expect

If Accepted: We will prioritize a fix and notify you as soon as the patch is deployed. With your permission, we will acknowledge your contribution to our security.

If Declined: We will provide a clear explanation if the report is determined to be a low-risk issue or intended behavior.

Disclosure: We ask that you do not share the vulnerability publicly until we have confirmed the fix is live.
